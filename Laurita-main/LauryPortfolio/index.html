Laury Garcia

Love Labour assembles a selection of mixed media artworks and a prose poem by Laury Garcia, a Latina queer artist who moved to Oslo in 2017 from Miami. The exhibition explores different forms of love, personal growth, and identity. A variety of participatory elements encourage visitors to contemplate their own ideas about identity and love whilst contributing to the exhibition if they desire.
